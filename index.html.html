My Favorite Hobby is
Reading Books

My hobby is reading. I always read before going to bed or any freetime I get. I usually read fiction books more than non-fiction books. Usually I read one series at a time. Reading all the books takes me to a new world! Worlds with magic, dragons, my favourite charecters, places,mazes and lots more. Its a world where you can open your mind and unleash your creativety. Some books also have movies which I watch. But the books usually explain a lot more than them. Readings these books makes us feel a lot of emotion. But also help us improve in a lot of things like vocabulary, imagination and createivety.
